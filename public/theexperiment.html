About The Experiment: Nature Edition

In this VR shooter game you are stuck in a abandoned and overgrown laboratory. Walk through (or explore) the laboratory to find a way out. But be carefull... There are robots that want to end your life! Can you repair the doors and escape the laboratory before these robots kill you?..

This game is made in 4 weeks with 2 developers and 5 artists
