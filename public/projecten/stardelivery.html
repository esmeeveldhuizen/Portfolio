Star Delivery

A game made in 5 weeks with 4 developers

Deliver through space in VR!

Step into the shoes of a space delivery driver, where you'll navigate through the stars and planets to deliver packages on time. With the immersive power of a racing seat and the Quest 3 VR headset, you're in for an exhilarating adventure. Race against the clock, dodge asteroids, and conquer challenging space routes, all while experiencing an ultra-realistic VR experience. Can you become the fastest, most reliable space delivery driver in the galaxy?
